NukeRun







Controls:

W, A, S and D - movement

Space Bar - jump

Mouse - looking

F - change perspective

Objective:

Oh no!!! You dropped your nuclear bomb. Don't worry, these things happen. But wait... it armed itself when you dropped it! And for some reason you dropped it on that island in the middle of the oasis in this lovely desert. You only have 30 seconds to disarm it!

Thankfully there appears to be a path of logs and crates to help you get across and disarm it. To disarm it just push the nuke into the water (try running into it to push it). Sorry... what did you just say? You can't swim! Well don't fall in the water then, otherwise we are all doomed.
